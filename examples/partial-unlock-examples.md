# Partial Unlock Examples: Points & Deficit System

## Example 1: "Beat Around the Bush" (Success Story)

### Component Details
- **Type**: Idiom (Tier 4)
- **Total Points**: 100 points
- **Partial Unlock**: 30% Day 1, 20% Day 3, 20% Day 7, 30% Day 14

### Timeline

**Day 1: Learn + Immediate Unlock**
- Learn: "beat around the bush"
- Immediate test: 3 questions, get 2/3 correct ✅
- **Unlock**: 30 points (30% of 100)
- **Withdraw**: Child withdraws 30 points immediately
- **Status**: 30 points withdrawn, 70 points locked

**Day 3: First Retention Test**
- Test: 1 question, get correct ✅
- **Unlock**: +20 points (20% of 100)
- **Withdraw**: Child withdraws 20 points
- **Status**: 50 points withdrawn, 50 points locked

**Day 7: Second Retention Test**
- Test: 1 question, get correct ✅
- **Unlock**: +20 points (20% of 100)
- **Withdraw**: Child withdraws 20 points
- **Status**: 70 points withdrawn, 30 points locked

**Day 14: Final Verification**
- Test: 1 question, get correct ✅
- **Unlock**: +30 points (30% of 100)
- **Withdraw**: Child withdraws 30 points
- **Status**: 100 points withdrawn, 0 points locked
- **Verified**: ✅ Component fully verified
- **Deficit**: 0 points (no deficit)

**Result**: Success! Child earned all 100 points with no deficit.

## Example 5: Conservative vs. Aggressive Strategy

### Component: "beat around the bush" (100 points)

### Conservative Strategy (Wait Until Day 14)

**Day 1-13**: No withdrawal
- **Risk**: Low (wait for full verification)
- **Available**: 0 points (locked)

**Day 14**: Pass final test ✅
- **Unlock**: 100 points (all at once)
- **Withdraw**: 100 points
- **Deficit**: 0 points
- **Result**: 100 points, no risk

### Aggressive Strategy (Withdraw Immediately)

**Day 1**: Withdraw 30 points
- **Risk**: High (30% deficit if fails)
- **Available**: 30 points withdrawn

**Day 3**: Withdraw 20 points
- **Risk**: Higher (50% deficit if fails)
- **Available**: 50 points withdrawn

**Day 7**: Withdraw 20 points
- **Risk**: Very high (70% deficit if fails)
- **Available**: 70 points withdrawn

**Day 14**: 
- **If PASS**: Withdraw 30 points, total 100 points ✅
- **If FAIL**: Deficit -70 points ❌

**Result**: Higher risk, but immediate gratification

## Key Takeaways

1. **Partial unlock**: 30% Day 1, 20% Day 3, 20% Day 7, 30% Day 14
2. **Early withdrawal**: Kids can withdraw immediately (faster gratification)
3. **Deficit risk**: If verification fails, deficit = withdrawn amount
4. **Deficit recovery**: Must re-verify or earn new points to clear
5. **Strategy choice**: Conservative (wait) vs. Aggressive (withdraw early)
6. **Account balance**: Tracks available, locked, and deficit points
